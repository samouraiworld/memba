## 1. Summary

The 2026-07-01 verified cross-perspective audit (PR #703) found that a post-v7.2.0
feature sprint had shipped demo-grade marketplace flows and wired-but-wrong realms, on
top of pre-existing backend-integrity and codegen gaps. Waves 0–2 of the remediation
plan are now **closed**: every Wave 0/1/2 task merged to `main` with tests, and the
backend is live on Fly with the hardened configuration. Wave 3 (CI/SAST/docs truth) is
in flight; Wave 4 (structural/UX) is continuous and non-gating.
