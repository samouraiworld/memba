## FAQ (link from banner)

**Q: Was my wallet compromised?**
No. This affects how the Memba server constructs the message your wallet signs at login. Nothing was extracted, leaked, or misused. The fix is preemptive — Memba is not yet running on multiple chains, but will be (betanet).

**Q: Do I need to update my wallet (Adena)?**
No, your wallet is fine. The change is server-side only.

**Q: Why am I being logged out?**
Because your existing 24-hour auth token was issued without a chain identifier. The new server requires one. Once you reconnect, your new token includes the chain (`test12`), and you won't be asked again.

**Q: What if I'm logged out repeatedly?**
That shouldn't happen. If it does, clear `localStorage` for `memba.samourai.app` and try again. If it persists, contact `@zxxma` on Discord.
